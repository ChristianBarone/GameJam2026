# GameJam2026
Welcome to Tetris Daltonic Survivor!

In this spin of the classic puzzle game, you must survive by dodging the pieces as they fall and try to squish you!

You have three colored masks at your disposal: red, green and blue! They can help you go through the blocks of their respective colors. You can even combine two! Be quick and know your RGBs!, three errors and you're out!

Survive for as long as you can and beat your highscore!
